Our Mission

The key idea of this extravaganza is to get more students and enthusiast involved in one of the most revolutionizing technologies of our time, AI and bring with them innovation and abolish the idea that AI is only for Grads/ Researchers.

A Little Learning Is Dangerous.

..drink deep or taste not the Pierian spring. MindHack! Summit is brought to you by a team of AI enthusiasts in collaboration with the AI Leaders who have leveraged it to create out-of-the box technologies that have a positive impact in the society.

We believe Artificial Intelligence is not just a few lines of code, but an artform altogether. Our Mission is change one s perspective on AI and the magic behind it. The MindHack! Summit aims to bring one of the finest AI practitioners who have used unconventional methods to build technologies that changed the world.
